# AI Chatbot for Small Businesses

A simple multilingual chatbot system designed for small restaurants, cafes, and local businesses.
The chatbot helps automate customer messages, answer frequently asked questions, collect reservation/order information, and reduce missed customer inquiries.

## Overview

This project was created as a personal learning and portfolio project to explore how AI and automation can support small businesses in customer communication.

The chatbot can be used as a website demo and can later be connected to platforms such as Facebook Page, Instagram, or other business messaging channels.

## Key Features

* Automated FAQ responses
* Multilingual support: Vietnamese, Japanese, and English
* Restaurant/shop information support:

  * Opening hours
  * Menu
  * Prices
  * Address
  * Reservation/order guidance
* Simple customer input form
* Customer information storage concept using Google Sheets or SQL database
* Fallback handling when the chatbot cannot understand a question
* Suitable for small restaurants, cafes, personal shops, and service businesses

## Use Cases

This chatbot is useful for:

* Restaurants receiving repeated questions from customers
* Cafes that want to reduce manual message replies
* Small shops that want a simple customer support assistant
* Portfolio demonstration for AI automation and web development

## How It Works

1. The customer opens the chatbot on a website or demo page.
2. The chatbot asks the customer to choose a language.
3. The customer asks a question or selects a suggested button.
4. The chatbot replies based on prepared business information.
5. If the chatbot cannot understand the message, it shows a fallback response and can guide the customer to contact staff.
6. Customer information can be saved for later management.

## Example Questions
